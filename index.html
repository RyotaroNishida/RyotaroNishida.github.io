About Me

A classically trained pianist proficient in both solo and ensemble performances. A competent and experienced music educator with an eye for musical detail and the ability to personalise lessons that cater to the needs and interests of each individual student. Seeking to obtain a position that offers both a challenge and opportunity for growth to benefit knowledge learnt and experience earned alongside professionals. For the last 7 years, I have been teaching piano for students aged 3 - 50. Accomplished in building trust and relationships between students, parents, and administrators.
